What to see in Argentina

Argentina is home to a wide range of terrains, from freezing cold mountaintops to sunny beaches. A mass producer of wine, meats, and soy, Argentina is a Latin American country that has a rich culture that is often overlooked. Argentina shares a border with Chile, divided by the Andes mountain range which runs all the way down to the southern tip of the country, called Tierra del Fuego. The vast plains in the coutnry are home to many types of ranches, mostly focusing on the production of beef. Argentina's capital, Buenos Aires, is often labelled by people as being the "Paris of South America." It's beautiful cobblestone streets have been the birth place of many revolutions, and the all famous argentine tango.

Bariloche is a cozy evergreen location with lakes and mountains

Iguazu Falls is the shared border between Argentina, Brazil, and Paraguay
